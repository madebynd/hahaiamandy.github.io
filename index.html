I'm a Hong Kong-born creative now based in Vancouver, BC. With a background spanning branding, motion graphics, and digital design, I've worked with publishers, cultural institutions and lifestyle brands across Asia and beyond.

My process is rooted in curiosity — I explore first, then distil. I believe in translating complex ideas into timeless, functional digital artifacts that respect the user's focus.
